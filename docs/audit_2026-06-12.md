## Honest standing limits (re-affirmed, not new)

- PCF certificates are conjecture-grade numerics (250 digits), not proofs.
- The flat-at-chance grok is indistinguishable from the cryptographic wall
  from outcome alone (near-fundamental); defenses are recommends-only
  verdicts, confidence grading with asymmetric patience, and the C3 exam.
- Sorting-network "rediscovery" is cost-exact, mostly wiring-different;
  naming up to untangling equivalence remains future recognizer work.
